Proactive Measures to Prioritise

by Dhanya Menon | Nov 9, 2024 | Cyber Security | 0 comments

Device Security

Your device is your responsibility. It could be the point of intrusion in your business. Your device could be used to cripple your business or as a gateway to intrude into someone else’s business.

It is very important to maintain proper security for your personal computer — whether it is a laptop or a desktop. Exploits like viruses, worms, Trojans, keyloggers, skimmers, and password filters could lead to data loss, theft, or compromised credentials.

A Few Things You Can Do:
  • Never leave your device unattended or in common areas accessible to others.
  • Keep a register of access whenever someone uses your device.
  • Back up your data regularly and keep multiple copies in different locations.
  • Memorize access passwords or PINs instead of writing them down.
  • Encrypt your data securely and follow strict access control policies.
  • Install updates regularly and use reliable antivirus software and firewalls.
  • Ensure gadgets are patched with the latest updates and never use pirated software.
  • Scan external devices before use and maintain password protection.
  • Remember that physical, technical, and personal security are all equally important.
