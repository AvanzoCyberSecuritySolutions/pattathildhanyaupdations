Life Balance Sheet

by Dhanya Menon | Nov 9, 2024 | Personal | 0 comments

I’ve been writing and rewriting this note many times, editing and refining it, yet I know it will never be perfect. No matter how much time I spend on it, it will always feel like a work in progress. Having crossed more than half of my journey, I write with the assumption—like most in my family who have celebrated their 80th—that I too will see many more years ahead.

Call this an evaluation or a note of gratitude? I am not sure. But the very act of putting my thoughts on paper has been a fascinating journey down memory lane.

The Negative Side

  • A failed marriage
  • Relatives who spoke ill behind my back
  • Loved ones who betrayed my trust
  • Friends who cheated

The Positive Side

  • Nature’s blessings, like devoted parents
  • Guardians who always had my best interests at heart
  • A son who mirrors me in so many ways
  • Children who are bright stars to look forward to
  • Friends who understand even my silence
  • Angels sent by God at the right moments
  • A loving partner to hold my hand—and occasionally nag—for the rest of my life

Material Achievements

  • Certificates that could fill an A4 sheet
  • A PhD in Dance
  • Dance productions curated to my taste
  • A national award
  • Published works as an author
  • A prestigious state assignment
  • The pride of keeping my “boat” afloat, rowed with dignity and style to date
